About Me

Hi there! I'm Tamoghna Das, a passionate data scientist from India, currently based in the UK. I thrive at the intersection of biological sciences and data, leveraging my background to bring a unique perspective to data analysis, modeling, and optimization.

With a PhD in Chemical Engineering (Loughborough), an M.Sc. in Biochemical Engineering (UCL), and an M.Sc. in Data Science (UEA), my multidisciplinary foundation fuels my aspiration to develop web applications for analyzing and visualizing complex biological and non-biological data.

Outside the professional sphere, I love traveling, photography, and videography. I'm excited to connect with like-minded professionals and explore new opportunities in the data science domain.
